DESCRIPTION

Are you an outgoing individual? Do you enjoy spending time meeting new people from all walks of life? Do you have a can-do attitude and give your all to any task? Are you looking for flexible hours to fit around your studies or freelance work? We are looking for someone like you to join our exciting, fast-growing business in a role at the heart of what we do.

This role would suit an ambitious undergraduate or post-graduate looking for well-paid work and flexible hours who understands the core tenets of customer service and hospitality. You will be the face of the business. Are you up for the challenge?

We welcome 1000s of guests (often tourists) to London every year - our team of greeters work with us to provide an in-person check-in service to all our guests.

The role includes:

  • ensuring the property is up to scratch prior to guest arrival

  • giving the guests a tour of the property

  • answering their questions, and resolving any problems they might have with the property.

  • liaising with the main office about check-ins and guest queries

You will also have the opportunity to perform a number of other ad-hoc tasks for an additional fee (e.g. changing light bulbs, receiving deliveries at the property, cutting new keys)

REQUIREMENTS

  • Always put the guest first and make them feel welcome in their new short-term rental

  • Be warm, sociable, well-presented and energetic: guests love a greeter with a bit of personality!

  • Be a problem-solver who can handle tricky situations. Sometimes things don’t go to plan, so we need someone who can think on their feet

  • Knows London well and is passionate about providing a great hospitality experience

  • Be team player who’s happy to work with our main office (London HQ)

BENEFITS

About The London Residents Club:

The London Residents Club (LRC), is a short-term rental management company with a strong focus on guest hospitality and our service to our client owners. In essence, we help property owners let their property through platforms such as The Plum Guide, Airbnb and Booking.com, achieving desirable yields while retaining the flexibility to stay in their home whenever they like. On the guest side, we provide unique, characterful, well-located properties with all the comforts of a hotel.

We are a young, fast-growing company and have further ambitions for expansion over the next two years. In our 3 years of trading, we have looked after 100s of properties, acquired another company and have built a team of 40+ staff. If you would like to join a small team in a growing business, with exciting prospects, this could be the startup for you!

Sales Manager Role Purpose:

To own the entire client acquisition process, to grow The London Residents Club’s portfolio of prime London homes at a sustainable rate. We want to scale the company while still maintaining our boutique feel and high level of service.
