Our Story

In 2013 we (Tom and Will) left our corporate jobs with the intentions of becoming full-time entrepreneurs. We brainstormed a number of ideas and even tried a few out. In the end, we decided to head back to our roots in the Berkshire countryside and set up an apple juicing operation in an effort to prove money does actually grow on trees… the business was a formative experience, full of highs and lows but we felt like its potential was limited.

We turned our gaze to the London property market - we met with agents, developers, industry experts, contractors and potential clients. From our research, it was clear that property management and specifically short let management was a nascent market brimming with opportunity. We set out with one simple task: to create the best short let management company in London. Three years later that is still our aim and we believe we’re not far off achieving that goal. We’re constantly innovating and seeking improvement and we will never rest on our laurels.

Guest Vetting & Deposits

Only verified, well-reviewed guests get to stay at your home and our greeters are well trained to inform the guests of our house rules. Security deposits are also taken for peace of mind.

An in person check-in serves two purposes. It is the final stage of the vetting process and it provides a friendly introduction and tour for the guest - our greeters are trained to know the area and to be able to provide recommendations. Our Premium Members also benefit from a photo inventory and in-person check-out

Housekeeping
